Delicious specializes in Private Parties, Corporate and Press Events, Weddings and other celebrations for groups from 5 to 500. We feature four private event spaces perfect for any occasion, as well as the option to book the entire restaurant. Additionally, we can provide design services & décor, a flat-screen tv or projector for presentations, and arrange entertainment if suitable for your event.
