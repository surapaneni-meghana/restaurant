Who we are

Welcome to Delicious, our home located in Hyderabad, where we invite guests to celebrate life and share the modern and traditional cuisine. Our goal at Delicious is to unify our guest’s cultures and spirits with our own, expressing passion and generosity in the cuisine we share. The history of our team, of our cooks and chefs who have left their mark on our kitchen, and our serving staff and managers who have been ambassadors of hospitality.

Delicious is a place for family, the one you’re born into and the one you create around food, love and friendship. Our attentive staff ensures that your entire dining experience with us is stellar from beginning to end.

Whether part of a party of fifty or a party of two, your experience throughout our elegant, yet comfortable space is eventful. We are sharing our long held traditions with a modern contemporary twist. You will feel both close to home and apart of something new.
