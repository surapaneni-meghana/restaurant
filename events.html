Private Events

We’d love to host your next event. Delicious is perfect to hold private parties for a wide range of celebrations including birthdays, brunches, corporate and press events, rehearsal dinners and weddings, receptions, and group happy hours.

Accommodating groups from 5 to 500, delicious offers four private event spaces in addition to our main dining room, each unique and centered around your party’s individual needs. We also offer the option to rent out our entire restaurant. We aim to personalize your affairs with us, taking on special requests to make your event memorable.

THE BAR ROOM

Our upstairs private Bar Room features two rows of banquette seating, and rich woodwork with cutouts .For an extra special dinner, a single large chef's table for up to 24 guests can be configured in the center of the room.

Location: Second floor

Privacy Level: Private

Seated: up to 20

A/V: screen & projector, flat screen TV, personal music.

THE GRATIFY ROOM

Our upstairs private Gratify Room features banquette seating on two rows, a private restroom, an overhead view of the conifer, and it makes perfect for birthday parties, dinners and boardroom dinners alike. The pleasant atmosphere here is one of the best thing to organise events.

Location: Second floor

Privacy Level: Private

Seated: up to 40

A/V: screen & projector, microphone, personal music.

THE PATIO ROOM

Our open terrace room projects some pleasant breeze and live screening.This is perfect for birthday parties, dinners and a spectacular overhead view of the sky, city. The room is spread all over the terrace and up to 20 guests can be configured in the center of the room.

Location: Terrace

Privacy Level: Private

Seated: up to 40

A/V: screen & projector, microphone, personal music.

SEMI PRIVATE DINING ROOM

Beside of Main Dining room, this ground flood semi-private space is perfect for guests looking to enjoy the vibrancy of our Main Dining space in a less crowded setting. We can accommodate up to 65 seated in one space, 95 guests seated between the two spaces.

Location: First floor

Privacy Level: Semi-Private

Seated: up to 45

A/V: screen & projector, flat screen TV.
